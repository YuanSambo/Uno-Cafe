Our Story

Filipino coffee industry Philippines, has a history as rich as its flavour. In 1740, the first coffee tree was introduced in Lipa, Batangas by a Spanish Franciscan monk. Coffee was spread to the other parts of Batangas like Ibaan, Lemery, San Jose, Taal and Tanauan. Batangas owed much of its wealth to the coffee plantations in these areas.

Philippines is one of the last frontiers for specialty coffee farming, producing and serving. Now, we're seeing the dawn of homegrown coffee and its social impact not only on indiviudal Filipino farmers, but on their local communities as well. We have just started our story as a specialty coffee powerhouse.

Uno café was originally came from Sto. Tomas, Batangas. It was made from the native Filipino coffee that have different varieties that will love the Filipino taste. “It all start with Uno Café”, There's absolutely no denying that mornings are so much better with a sip of coffee. Our naturally-grown coffee is collected and shipped to various locations in the country.
